Meet our staff

Touch The Dom is a virtual music academy formed by a team of four musicians, who are dedicated to teaching how to play the guitar, piano and handpan.

Inno is a guitar teacher with more than 10 years of experience. He is also a doctor in music theory. And he is always willing to help his students.

Sonia is our pianist. She was a member of the Gregorian Orchestra for 8 years. And she specialized in musical arrangements for soundtracks.

Felix plays the handpan. He is a self-taught musician who learned to play on the street. Music was his salvation, thanks to it he overcame a severe depression.
