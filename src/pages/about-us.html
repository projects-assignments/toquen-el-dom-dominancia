About Us

Touch the Dom is a virtual music school that believes that everyone should have the opportunity to learn and create music. We offer a variety of online courses that cover a wide range of musical instruments and genres. Our courses are taught by experienced and passionate musicians who are committed to helping students of all levels reach their full potential.

Our Team

Our team is made up of experienced and passionate musicians who are committed to helping students of all levels reach their full potential. We are all passionate about music and believe that everyone should have the opportunity to learn and create music.

Our Story

Touch the Dom was founded by four friends who met in college. Aina and Inno played guitar, Sonia played the piano, and Felix played the handpan. The four of them formed a rock band together and played shows around town. After a few years, they decided to start a music school to share their love of music with others.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make music education accessible and affordable for everyone. We believe that music can be a powerful force for good in the world, and we want to help people of all ages and backgrounds experience the joy of creating music.

Our Courses

We offer a variety of online courses that cover a wide range of musical instruments and genres. Our courses are designed to be flexible and accessible, so you can learn at your own pace and on your own time.
